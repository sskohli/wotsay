Wotsay - Introduction

Background:
There are a number of different organizations who conduct surveys on a regular basis. These companies include Marketing Companies, Consulting Companies, News Channels, Political Parites etc. These companies hire third party companies to conduct these surveys. These companies go in the field and interact with individuals to conduct these surveys. These surveys are mostly taken on paper and then begins the arduous task of data entry and adding these surveys to a computer in order to analyze and make sense of the data.

Challenges: There are many challenges to the traditional method of conducting surveys:
  • Targeted Audience: Most surveys are targeted to a specific target group, like school going girls, middle class housewives, unemployed graduates, etc. It is very difficult to find this target group. The effort would involve time and money.
  • Effort: In any survey the effort in terms of manpower and money is quite high. Surveys need to be printed, distributed,  geographies identified, then individuals need to go in person to conduct these surveys. It' a mammoth exercise.
  • Unwilling Audience: There is no incentive to complete a survey. Why would an person who's late to office would stop to listen and answer stupid questions about him? With increasing crimes, housewives will not allow strangers to come to their house to fill a questionnaire.
  • Authenticity: Since there is so much effort required to fill a survey and the audience is not forthcoming, the individuals conducting the survey themselves enter the answers or just get them randomly filled.
Wotsay to the rescue: Wotsay! is an incentive based mobile/web platform where the registered users willingly answer questions in lieu of benefits.
Advantages:
  • Handpicked Audience: When a user registers with Wotsay, he/she provides valuable demographic information. Companies can thus handpick their audience based on their criteria and the survey would be delivered specifically to the targeted audience.
  • Minimal Effort: Companies would not need to go door to door to conduct surveys. All they need to do is create a questionnaire, choose an audience and deliver the survey. They decide how much they want to spend on a campaign and the survey would be delivered accordingly.
  • Incentivized Audience: The audience can check and respond to surveys at their own convenience, whenever they're free. Since there is an incentive for them to answer, they're more than willing to answer and also advertise the product.
  • Authenticity: Every user is registered on the system. Companies can cross-check and analyze the results based on this user data. No more random and incomplete entries.
  • Live Reports: The companies can view the response of their surveys immediately and in real time. They no longer have to wait for the survey to be administered, collected and then fed into the system to view the results. Numerous Graphical reports can be created to analyze and categorize the response data.

Who would be our initial target audience?
To introduce and spread our app we need an audience who would be willing to use our app and will be more than happy to spread. Now we would be giving incentive on profile creation and reference.
In India, who will this dynamic audience be. We envision our initial target audience would be:
  • Tech savvy students, who own a smartphone and/or a laptop. They always want to try out new things and if there's an incentive, even better. These also have closed knit communities and would easily spread the app.
  • House Wives: These have a lot of free time and are very active on their smartphones, whatsapp, facebook. They would definitely try out something if there is an incentive and will spread the word to their peers.
  • Middle Class Salesmen: These are metro train commuting individuals who have a lot of free time during their commute and would love to earn a quick buck.
  • Freshers and lower level executives: These are fresh out of college and are just starting or have been in their jobs for a couple of years. They have limited pay but high dreams and wouldn't mind earning something on the extra.

Where would be target these audience or where do we advertise?
In order to spread our word effectively, we need to understand our target audience and target them accordingly.
  • Students: We need to advertise near schools/colleges/tuition centers. Probably banners or on facebook?
  • House Wives: We need to target grocery stores, malls, temples, during afternoon tv shows, kitty party venues.
  • Middle Class Salesmen: We need to advertise around metro stations, and on radio as this audience listen to radio during their commute.
  • Freshers and lower level executives: Facebook, near and around companies, banners.
Incentives:
  • Initially, we wouldn't want to involve any monetary incentive as it would involve a payment gateway, etc. We would want the incentive to be lucrative for the user and would want the users to continuously/repeatedly use our app.
  • We surveyed and most of the target audience would be interested in getting free talktime.
  • We will give free talktime, Rs 50 worth on profile creation, Rs 20 for referral and Rs 20-100 for a filling a survey.
